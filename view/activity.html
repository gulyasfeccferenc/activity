Help

It's a simple platform to play some Activity.

For a new game just simply

  • Click on the refresh button in the bottom-right corner
  • It will show on a dialog how you have to perform that word (Act, Draw or Describe)
  • Below that you can see the actual word
  • After you memorized the word, just close the dialog (with the x or just click anywhere)
  • Click on the play button will start the counter.
